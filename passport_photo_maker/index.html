Frequently Asked Questions

Is this passport photo maker really free?
Yes, completely free! No subscriptions, no watermarks, no hidden charges. All processing happens in your browser.
Is my photo uploaded to a server?
No! All processing — including AI background removal — happens entirely on your device. Your photo never leaves your browser.
Which countries' passport photo sizes are supported?
We support India (3×4 cm, 3.5×4.5 cm), USA (2×2 inch), UAE, EU standard, Aadhaar, PAN card, and many more. Use Custom size for any country.
How does AI background removal work?
Our tool uses a machine learning model that runs entirely in your browser via WebAssembly. No data is sent to any server.
